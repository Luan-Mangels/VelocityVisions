Hello & Welcome!


Velocity Visions is a digital portfolio website I’ve created to assist in building an online presence for myself where I can display my personal interest in cars through the use of photography.

Over the last few years, I’ve occasionally experimented with photography as a new and different way to express my enjoyment and love for cars and the culture around it. Throughout my journey my skill as a photographer and the quality of photography that I can produce have improved greatly. The two types of photography that I’ve had the most experience in are video game photography and photography at events. These events range from track days, car meets, drag racing events, etc.

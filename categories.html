I've divided up my photography into two main categories. The first category “Virtual” consists of photos that have been taken from games using their photo modes. The other category, "Event", contains photos that I've taken in person at multiple events. Out of the two, I'd say the first category has the better photos due to me being more experienced and experimental with that type of photography and usually had the ability to tweak it to my liking completely start to finish.

Contact Information


Hi, my name is Luan Mangels and if you couldn’t already tell, I hold a deep passion for cars and everything around them.


I’ve always been fascinated by these incredible pieces of automotive engineering and very much consider then to be a form of artwork in their own right; from the way they look to how the perform or even just the sound they produce. I find there to be a captivating sense of freedom in the way someone can discover/express their interest, emotions or even a piece of their personality through cars and car culture.

Hopefully you’ve already helped yourself around and took an interest in some of the work I’ve done and have found my photography pieces interesting enough to want to know more about me or maybe some of the stories behind them.


Feel free to contact me for queries using this e-mail address: mangel1@student.eit.ac.nz
